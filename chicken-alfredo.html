Chicken Alfredo


Delight your taste buds with our Chicken Alfredo
— a symphony of savory flavors in every forkful.
Enjoy this creamy comfort food at its finest!

5 out of 5 Stars



Prep Time

Cook Time

Total Time


10 min

20 min

30 min


Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the sliced chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer.
  6. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  7. Add the cooked chicken back to the skillet and let it simmer in the sauce for a few minutes.
  8. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve the Alfredo sauce and chicken over the cooked fettuccine pasta.
  10. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and enjoy your delicious Chicken Alfredo!
