Barbeque Wings


Satisfy your cravings with our irresistible Barbecue
Wings — a perfect blend of smoky, sweet, and
spicy flavors that will leave you wanting more!

5 out of 5 Stars



Prep Time

Cook Time

Total Time


10 min

45 min

55 min


Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Chopped green onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, toss the chicken wings with vegetable oil, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Arrange the seasoned wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake the wings in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes or until crispy and cooked through.
  5. While the wings are baking, prepare the barbecue sauce by mixing barbecue sauce, honey, soy sauce, and apple cider vinegar in a bowl.
  6. Once the wings are done, transfer them to a large bowl.
  7. Pour the barbecue sauce mixture over the wings and toss to coat them evenly.
  8. Return the sauced wings to the baking sheet and bake for an additional 5-7 minutes until the sauce is sticky and caramelized.
  9. Garnish the barbecue wings with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
  10. Serve your delicious Barbecue Wings and enjoy the finger-licking goodness!
