Ingredients

  • 1 packet of frozen Acai puree (unsweetened)
  • 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, or raspberries)
  • 1/2 banana, frozen
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or agave syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • Toppings: Granola, sliced fresh fruits (such as banana, strawberry, kiwi), shredded coconut, chia seeds, and nuts.

Instructions

  1. Thaw a packet of frozen Acai under warm water and break it into smaller pieces.
  2. In a blender, combine Acai pieces, frozen mixed berries, frozen banana, and almond milk.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Adjust sweetness with honey or agave syrup (optional), and blend again.
  5. Pour the blended Acai mixture into a bowl.
  6. Top with granola, sliced fresh fruits, shredded coconut, chia seeds, and nuts.
  7. Serve and enjoy your delicious Acai Bowl!
