Vegan Apple Crumble


Deliciously cruelty-free, our Vegan Apple Crumble invites
you to savor the warm embrace of cinnamon-spiced apples
nestled beneath a golden, plant-powered crumble. Pure
indulgence, without compromise.

5 out of 5 Stars



Prep Time

Bake Time

Total Time


15 min

40 min

55 min


Ingredients

  • 6 cups peeled and sliced apples (such as Granny Smith)
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan butter, melted
  • Vegan vanilla ice cream (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with lemon juice, granulated sugar, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg. Transfer the apple mixture to a baking dish.
  3. In the same bowl, combine rolled oats, all-purpose flour, brown sugar, and salt. Pour melted vegan butter over the mixture and stir until crumbly.
  4. Sprinkle the oat mixture evenly over the apples in the baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 40 minutes or until the topping is golden brown, and the apples are tender.
  6. Allow the vegan apple crumble to cool slightly before serving.
  7. Serve warm, optionally with a scoop of vegan vanilla ice cream.
  8. Enjoy your homemade Vegan Apple Crumble!
