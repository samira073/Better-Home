Salmon Bowl


Elevate your taste experience with our Asian-Inspired
Salmon Bowl — a harmony of succulent salmon, flavorful
Asian spices, and wholesome ingredients that deliver
a bowlfulof culinary delight.

4 out of 5 Stars



Prep Time

Cook Time

Total Time


15 min

15 min

30 min


Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine rice, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas)
  • Green onions, sliced (for garnish)
  • esame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine jasmine rice and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes or until rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey, minced garlic, and grated ginger to create the sauce.
  3. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  4. Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet. Season with salt and black pepper. Brush the fillets with half of the prepared sauce.
  5. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes or until cooked through.
  6. While the salmon is baking, heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add mixed vegetables and sauté until crisp-tender.
  7. Fluff the cooked jasmine rice and divide it among bowls.
  8. Top with sautéed vegetables and baked salmon fillets.
  9. Drizzle the remaining sauce over the bowls.
  10. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds.
  11. Serve and enjoy your delicious Asian-inspired Salmon Rice Bowl!
