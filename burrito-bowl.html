Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges (for serving)
  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add diced chicken to the skillet and season with chili powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper. Cook until the chicken is cooked through.
  3. In serving bowls, assemble cooked brown rice, black beans, corn, cherry tomatoes, avocado slices, red onion, and the cooked chicken.
  4. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges.
  5. Optionally, add a dollop of sour cream or Greek yogurt.
  6. Serve and enjoy your delicious and wholesome Chicken Burrito Bowl!
