Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup cooked and crumbled sausage
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or your choice)
  • 1 medium-sized flour tortilla
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Salsa, sour cream, or guacamole for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, cook and crumble the sausage until browned and cooked through.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Push the sausage to one side of the skillet and pour the whisked eggs into the other side.
  4. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked and combine them with the cooked sausage.
  5. Warm the tortillas in the skillet or microwave.
  6. Spoon the egg and sausage mixture onto the center of each tortilla.
  7. Optional: Add shredded cheese on top of the egg and sausage mixture.
  8. Roll each tortilla into a burrito, folding in the sides as you go.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your hearty Breakfast Burrito!
