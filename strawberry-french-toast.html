Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Butter for cooking
  • Fresh strawberries, sliced (for topping)
  • Maple syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon until well combined.
  2. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a pat of butter to coat the surface.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are well-coated.
  4. Place the coated bread slices on the hot skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Once the French toast is cooked, transfer it to a plate.
  6. Top the French toast with sliced fresh strawberries.
  7. Optionally, drizzle with maple syrup for added sweetness.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
