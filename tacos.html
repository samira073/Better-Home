Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank or skirt steak
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Corn tortillas
  • Toppings: Chopped onions, fresh cilantro, salsa, lime wedges

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together olive oil, orange juice, lime juice, minced garlic, cumin, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Place the steak in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over it. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  3. Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the steak from the marinade and grill for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  5. Heat the corn tortillas on the grill or in a pan.
  6. Assemble your tacos by placing slices of carne asada on each tortilla.
  7. Top with chopped onions, fresh cilantro, salsa, and serve with lime wedges.
  8. Serve and enjoy your delicious Carne Asada Tacos!
