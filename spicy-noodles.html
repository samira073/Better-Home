Spicy Inferno Noodles


Ignite your taste buds with our Spicy Inferno Noodles
— a fiery fusion of bold spices and intense heat, promising
a tantalizing adventure in every spicy slurp.

5 out of 5 Stars



Prep Time

Cook Time

Total Time


10 min

15 min

25 min


Ingredients

  • 8 oz Asian noodles (such as ramen or udon)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions, sliced (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Asian noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a wok or large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add broccoli and bell pepper to the wok. Stir-fry until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, Sriracha sauce, hoisin sauce, and brown sugar.
  6. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the wok and stir to coat.
  7. Add the cooked noodles to the wok. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
  8. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds.
  9. Serve your Spicy Inferno Noodles immediately and enjoy the bold flavors!
